Aim of this program is to evaluate any given expression of numbers
according to BODMAS rule (this is priority order in BODMAS: brackets, 
division, multiplication, addition and subtraction).

The format of the input and output is shown below.

 Sample Input:
 -------------
 (60/30)*25+100-60
 
 Sample Output:	
 --------------
 2
 50
 150
 90
